Service Name: Other Banks PSID Payment

Banks that support PSID payments:
- HBL (Habib Bank Limited) - via HBL Mobile App and Internet Banking
- UBL (United Bank Limited) - via UBL Digital App
- MCB Bank - via MCB Mobile App
- Bank Alfalah - via Alfalah Mobile App
- Meezan Bank - via Meezan Mobile Banking
- Allied Bank - via ABL Mobile App
- Standard Chartered - via SC Mobile App
- Askari Bank - via Askari Mobile Banking
- Bank of Punjab - via BOP Smart Banking
- Faysal Bank - via Faysal Digibank

General Steps for Most Banks (Internet Banking):
1. Log in to your bank’s internet banking portal, microfinance wallet, or mobile banking app.
2. Go to “Bill Payments,” “1LINK Payments,” or “PSID Payment.”
3. Select “1 Bill” or “Invoice/Voucher” as the payment type.
4. Enter your 24-digit PSID.
5. Review the details (institution, amount, due date) carefully.
6. Confirm the payment using your PIN or OTP.
7. Save or take a screenshot of the receipt/confirmation for your records.

Important Notes:
- The exact menu path may differ between banks but the PSID entry step is the same
- Ensure your bank account has sufficient funds before initiating payment
- Some banks may charge a small service fee for bill payments - check with your bank
- Contact your bank's helpline if you cannot find the PSID payment option
